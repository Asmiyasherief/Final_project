@ College of Engineering Perumon – June 17, 2025

Sustainable Development: Powering a Greener Future

About the Hackathon

Hackathon 2025 is a 24-hour coding and innovation event aimed at solving real-world challenges through technology, with a focus on Sustainable Development. Organized by the College of Engineering Perumon, the event invites students to collaborate, brainstorm, and develop impactful tech solutions.

Themes may include clean energy, climate action, smart cities, waste management, and sustainable agriculture. It’s open to students from all backgrounds—whether you're a developer, designer, or just someone with a big idea.

Participants will get a chance to work with mentors, attend mini workshops, and showcase their solutions in front of an expert panel. It's an opportunity to learn, network, and make a difference through technology.
